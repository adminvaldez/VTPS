Scheduling Options

Residential Scheduling

Most residential services are scheduled during early morning hours. This allows optimal drying time and reduces household traffic during cleaning.

Commercial Scheduling

Commercial properties may request after-hours or late-night service to minimize customer disruption. We coordinate directly with property managers.

Payment & Booking Options

Online Payment to Secure Appointment

Customers may choose to pay online at booking to reserve their service date. This guarantees your time slot and confirms scheduling.

Custom / Unique Jobs

For large commercial projects or unique scheduling needs, appointments may be reserved without upfront payment. Final invoicing occurs after completion unless otherwise agreed.

Payment is due upon completion unless prepaid at booking.
